# Substrate Management in Blueberry Container Farming

Substrate selection plays a critical role in commercial blueberry cultivation systems.

Blueberries generally require:

- acidic growing conditions
- stable moisture retention
- adequate aeration
- consistent drainage performance

---

## Common Substrate Materials

Commercial growers may use:

- coco coir
- peat moss
- pine bark
- perlite blends

The ideal substrate depends on climate conditions, irrigation strategy, and container design.

---

## Oxygen Retention and Drainage

Excessive water retention may reduce oxygen availability in the root zone.

Modern container systems often focus on balancing moisture retention with sufficient drainage and airflow.
